About The Doctor

Meet Dr. Tony

Growing up in the Chicago area, I always knew I wanted to make a meaningful impact on people’s lives. My parents taught me that what you put into the world is what you receive, and that belief guided me toward a career in healthcare. I chose this path to help hard‑working individuals improve their health, mobility, and overall quality of life. Life University aligned perfectly with my passion for chiropractic care. There, I deepened my understanding of the body’s innate ability to heal when supported with the right conditions and proper alignment. At Toney Family Chiropractic, our mission is simple: educate, empower, and heal. Through targeted chiropractic adjustments, we focus not only on relieving pain but on restoring long‑term wellness. When you trust us with your care, you’re choosing a team committed to helping you move, feel, and live better.

Expect clear communication, thoughtful treatment, and a plan built for lasting progress.
